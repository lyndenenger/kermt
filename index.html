Direct Billing

Approved provider for common coverage paths.

Katherine is licenced under CCHPBC and an active member of RMTBC, and an approved service provider for ICBC, MSP, RCMP, VAC, and many extended health plans.

  • ICBC

    Coverage related to a motor vehicle accident claim in British Columbia. RMT may be covered when treatment is approved or eligible under the ICBC claim.

  • MSP

    Medical Services Plan of BC. This is provincial health coverage. MSP generally does not broadly cover RMT for most people, but limited supplementary benefits may apply for eligible low-income residents.

  • RCMP

    Coverage for Royal Canadian Mounted Police members, usually through federal or occupational health benefits. RMT coverage depends on the member's plan rules.

  • VAC

    Veterans Affairs Canada. RMT may be covered for eligible veterans if it relates to approved benefits or treatment programs.

  • Extended Health

    Private or employer-sponsored benefits, such as Canada Life, Sun Life, Manulife, Blue Cross, and similar plans. RMT is commonly covered up to an annual maximum, often requiring the therapist to be registered.
